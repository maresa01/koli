## Purpose

 it must created for this problem "for Many children aged 7-9 have difficulty concentrating immediately when starting a homework assignment or mental work, because their brain is still in the developmental stage. The part of the brain that helps a child sit, control themselves and think for a long time is not yet fully formed, and attention is easily distracted by external sounds and movements. Because of this, children often start tasks late, get distracted early and make more mistakes at first. If this is not understood by an adult, the child may feel “distracted” or “incompetent”, “lazy”, which affects his self-confidence and desire to learn.".  The transition from play to work ceases to be a struggle because there is now a ritual between them. The brain is warmed up. The child is ready.
